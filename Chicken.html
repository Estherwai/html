Serving 4

Quick and easy coated chicken recipe for busy people on the go!

Nutrition Facts Per Serving: 291 calories; protein 31.4g; carbohydrates 3.4g; fat 16.3g; cholesterol 109.3mg; sodium 320.3mg. Full Nutrition

Ingredients

  • 1 cup crushed cornflakes cereal
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 8 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Preparation

  1. Step 1 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Step 2 In a medium bowl, mix the cornflake crumbs, Parmesan cheese and Cheddar cheese.
  3. Step 3 Dip the chicken breasts in the melted butter, and roll them in the cornflake crumb mixture. Place chicken in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
  4. Step 4 Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes, until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.
